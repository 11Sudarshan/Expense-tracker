FAQs

1. How?
The website is designed such that all the data entered is backed up in the form of an expense table. As long as the user logs in to his account, he/she can view the expenses entered previously.
2. Where can I check my individual expenses on the Financial Tracker?
Once an expense is entered in the tracker, it gets automatically stored in the expense table. It can further be edited or deleted as per the user's request. This feature has been added to allow the user to view and analyze all his daily expenses individually.
3. Do I have link my bank account with the website??
That won't be needed as we don't deal with online as well as physical transactions. This website is solely for the purpose of tracking the users' expenses to help them get an idea of their spendings and savings which will enable them to get a better overview of how to manage their budget.
4. How secure is the Financial Tracker?
Financial Tracker is secure to use as each user has their own credentials to log into their accounts that are authenticated. Moreover, no bank information is needed to use Financial Tracker.
5. Does the expense table reset everyday?
Financial Tracker is a record or history of all the user's previous expenses. Hence, the previous day's data will not be erased. The current data entered by the user will just be added to the table. This allows the users to look back and scrutinize their expenses and work on their savings.
